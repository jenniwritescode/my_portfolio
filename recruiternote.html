A Note for Recruiters, Head Hunters and HR People

While I am not currently looking for a new role, perhaps you have an absolutely amazing position I would be interested in!

To make the entire process as low effort as possible and not a waste of time for both of us, here are a few things to consider when contacting me regarding a position:

  • Fully and permanently remote, W-2 positions only.
  • I live in Chicago and do not wish to relocate.
  • I do not reply to mass messages.
  • I will not be able to consider a role without a company name, full job description, level, and base pay rate.
  • Please read my resume to ensure the role matches my skill set and experience level.

After reviewing the above points, if you believe I would be interested in what you have to offer, please reach out and I would be happy to discuss further.

Thank you for your consideration!
